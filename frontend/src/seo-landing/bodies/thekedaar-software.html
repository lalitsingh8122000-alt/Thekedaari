Thekedaar Software me kya kya milega?

Construction business me sabse badi problem hoti hai scattered data. Alag diary me attendance, alag notebook me kharcha aur alag dimag me udhaar ya payment. Thekedaari in sab ko ek digital system me convert karta hai jisse aapko real-time control milta hai.

👷

Worker Attendance

Roz ki attendance, half-day, full-day, site-wise ya project-wise entry. Purani register system ko simple digital attendance me convert karo.

💰

Labour Payment & Udhaar

Kis worker ko kitna dena hai, kitna advance diya, kitna balance bacha — sab kuch clear ledger ke saath.

📦

Material & Site Kharcha

Cement, steel, plumbing, transport, petty cash aur daily site expense ka smart हिसाब. Loss kaha ho raha hai, turant samajh aata hai.

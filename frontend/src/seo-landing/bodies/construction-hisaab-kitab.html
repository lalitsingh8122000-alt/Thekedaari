Why site accounting often goes wrong

Construction projects me hisaab alag alag jagah likha jata hai: diary, chits, Excel, WhatsApp ya yaadash. Isliye month end par exact numbers nikalna mushkil ho jata hai. Thekedaari ek disciplined digital record deta hai jisse aapko site ke asli numbers milte hain.

  • Daily entry discipline
  • Expense categorization
  • Worker wage tracking
  • Material record support
  • Cash flow visibility
  • Project wise profit view
